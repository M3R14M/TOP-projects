Meatloaf

Description

An easy recipe for a delicious meatloaf, suitable to feed up to 8 people.

Ingredients

  • 1½ pounds of ground beef
  • 1 egg
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 1 cup of milk
  • 1 cup of dried bread crumbs
  • 2 tablespoons of brown sugar
  • 2 tablespoons of prepared mustard
  • ⅓ cup of ketchup
  • Salt and peper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F or 175 degrees C.
  2. In a large bowl, combine the beef, egg, onion, milk and bread crumbs. Season with salt and pepper to taste and place in a lightly greased 9x5-inch loaf pan.
  3. In a seperate small bowl, combine the brown sugar, mustard and ketchup. Mix well and pour over the meatloaf.
  4. Bake for 1 hour.
